Tiftown

Modeling and Fashion News Site

The goal of TifTown is to help models learn more about the fashion industry through the eyes of Tiffany Nelson. She has been in the field for almost a decade and her mission is to help newcomers make the most of their experience. Our job was to help create a clear image of Tiffany Nelson as an individual. The website can be seen here. It is updatable by the end user so she can update things when she needs to in a pinch.
